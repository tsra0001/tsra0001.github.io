Why HBO-ICT: My Study Choice Journey

A decision that combined passion for technology with career aspirations

September 8, 2025 Study Choice

The Journey Begins

Choosing HBO-ICT at HZ University of Applied Sciences was a decision that combined my passion for technology with my career aspirations. After completing my vocational education in Computer Programming and Innovation, I realized I wanted to deepen my knowledge and broaden my horizons in the ICT field. My goal is to become a Full-Stack Software Developer, working on challenging projects that combine technical skills with creativity.

Why HZ University of Applied Sciences?

The decision to choose HZ University wasn't made lightly. Several factors influenced my choice:

Practical Focus: The HBO-ICT program at HZ offers the perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application. Unlike purely academic programs, this curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience with real-world projects, which aligns perfectly with my learning style and career goals.

Industry Connections: HZ has strong partnerships with local and international ICT companies, providing excellent internship and job placement opportunities. This network is invaluable for someone like me who wants to seamlessly transition from education to a professional career.

Modern Curriculum: The program stays current with industry trends, covering emerging technologies like cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and modern development frameworks. This ensures that graduates are prepared for the rapidly evolving ICT landscape.

Study Choice Activities

Before making my final decision, I engaged in several study choice activities that helped solidify my choice:

Open Days and Information Sessions: I attended multiple open days at HZ, where I spoke with current students, faculty members, and recent graduates. Their insights into the program structure, workload, and career outcomes were invaluable in my decision-making process.

Campus Tours: Visiting the facilities gave me a tangible sense of the learning environment. The modern computer labs, collaborative spaces, and access to cutting-edge technology convinced me that this was where I wanted to spend the next few years.

Shadowing Current Students: I had the opportunity to shadow a third-year student for a day, observing lectures, participating in group projects, and experiencing the daily routine of an HBO-ICT student. This gave me realistic expectations about what to expect.

Motivation Behind My Choice

My motivation for choosing HBO-ICT stems from both personal passion and career objectives:

Technical Passion: Since discovering programming in vocational school, I've been fascinated by the power of code to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions. The HBO-ICT program allows me to explore this passion at a deeper level while learning about system design, software architecture, and emerging technologies.

Career Aspirations: My ultimate goal is to become a Full-Stack Software Developer, capable of working on both front-end user interfaces and back-end systems. The comprehensive curriculum covers all aspects of software development, from database design to user experience, giving me the well-rounded education I need.

Innovation and Creativity: What excites me most about ICT is its creative potential. Software development isn't just about writing code; it's about designing elegant solutions, creating intuitive user experiences, and building systems that make people's lives better. The project-based learning approach at HZ encourages this creative problem-solving mindset.

Future Perspectives

After completing this study, I envision several potential career paths:

Full-Stack Developer: My primary goal is to work as a full-stack developer in a dynamic company where I can contribute to meaningful projects. I'm particularly interested in web application development and would love to work on applications that serve thousands of users.

Software Architect: In the long term, I aspire to take on more strategic roles, such as software architect, where I can design system architectures and guide technical decisions for complex projects.

Entrepreneurship: The combination of technical skills and business knowledge gained through HBO-ICT could prepare me to start my own tech company or join a startup in a technical leadership role.

Specialization Opportunities: The ICT field offers numerous specialization paths. Whether it's artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud computing, or mobile development, the foundation provided by HBO-ICT will allow me to pursue advanced specializations based on evolving interests and market demands.

Preparing for the Dynamic World of Software Development

This program offers exactly what I need to succeed in the dynamic world of software development. The emphasis on both technical skills and soft skills like project management, teamwork, and communication prepares graduates for the collaborative nature of modern software development.

The international environment at HZ, with students from diverse backgrounds, mirrors the global nature of the ICT industry. This exposure to different perspectives and approaches to problem-solving will be invaluable in my future career.

Moreover, the focus on continuous learning and adaptation aligns with the reality of the ICT field, where technologies evolve rapidly, and professionals must constantly update their skills to remain relevant.

Looking Forward

As I embark on this HBO-ICT journey, I'm excited about the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. The combination of my vocational programming background and the comprehensive education I'll receive at HZ positions me well for a successful career in software development.

I'm particularly looking forward to the internship opportunities, where I can apply classroom learning to real-world projects and gain valuable industry experience. These experiences will not only enhance my technical skills but also help me build a professional network that will be crucial for my career development.

The choice to pursue HBO-ICT represents more than just an educational decision; it's an investment in my future and a step toward becoming the kind of developer who can make a meaningful impact in the technology industry.
