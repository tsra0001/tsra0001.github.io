Q1

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Connect to the HZ Wi-Fi, open the HZ print portal (usually print.hz.nl or via the HZ intranet), upload your file or send it to the network printer using the provided drivers. Authenticate with your HZ username/password at the printer and collect your print job. If you need drivers, visit the IT pages or use the web print portal.

Q2

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Use any campus multifunction device (scan/copy machine). Choose 'Scan to e-mail' or 'Scan to USB' on the device. For e-mail, enter your HZ e-mail address and the scanned PDF will arrive in your inbox. Some devices also support scanning to a network folder or the web scan portal; check the instructions on the machine or IT intranet.

Q3

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Go to the HZ web shop page linked from the intranet, log in with your HZ account, add the item or event to your cart and proceed to checkout. Choose invoice or payment method offered (student/staff options may differ). After purchase you'll receive confirmation by e-mail. If an event requires registration, follow the event page steps and pay if prompted.

Q4

How can you book a project space?

Open the HZ room booking system (check the intranet link or 'Room booking' app). Search for available project spaces on the date and time you need, select the slot, and confirm the booking. You may be asked to add attendees or a purpose. If you have special equipment needs, contact Facility Services or the space administrator.

Q5

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Park only in designated HZ bays. Follow signage and any permit rules (staff/students may need a permit or to register a plate). Pay at the parking terminal or use the HZ parking app if required. Observe time limits and accessibility spaces. For long-term or special parking requests contact Campus Services.
